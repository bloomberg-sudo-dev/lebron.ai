# March 27, 2026 - Project Direction & AI Video Breakthrough

## Key Decision: Real-Time Diffusion Video Platform

**Steering toward:** Building a real-time generative video platform with temporal consistency, NOT just an avatar API.

**Why:** 10x larger TAM ($500B+ vs $80B). Defensible research moat. Can be sold to gaming, content, advertising, metaverse companies.

### Project Overview
- **Goal:** Solve temporal consistency in real-time diffusion (frames don't flicker between generations)
- **Target latency:** 100-300ms end-to-end (feels natural for live video)
- **Quality:** Photorealistic 
- **Adaptiveness:** Full LLM-powered responses (not pre-generated)
- **Use cases:** AI avatars, game NPCs, content generation, dynamic ads, metaverse avatars

### Technology Stack Decision
**Chosen approach:** 3D Gaussian Splatting + Predictive Pre-Generation + Real-time Diffusion with Temporal Attention

**Why not alternatives:**
- Pre-generated clips only (limited adaptiveness)
- Standard FOMM/LivePortrait (too slow, not innovative enough)
- Real-time SD vanilla (consistency nightmare)

- Pricing: $0.01-0.05 per video minute OR $10k+/month per dedicated customer
